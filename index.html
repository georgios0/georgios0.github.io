Research Interests

I have extensive experience in Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Deep Learning research. I am currently working at Microsoft as a Principal S/W Engineer. I am a member of the Custom Vision team in the Cognitive Services organization working on Image and Video search. Previously, I was leading the Neural Network hardware accelerator software team at Samsung. I am actively working on algorithms that accelerate and compress Neural Networks. During my PhD thesis, I dealt with video understanding, texture/structure partition, texture compression, synthesis and video compression. I have also worked on problems related to video segmentation, scene flow and texture segmentation. I have been exposed through research or courses to Machine Learning, Video and Image Processing and Convex Optimization.
