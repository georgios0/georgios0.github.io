Research Interests

I have extensive experience in Computer Vision and Machine Learning research. I am actively working in problems related to Deep Learning and I am interested in applying it in novel domains. My PhD thesis deals with video understanding, texture representation and synthesis, structure encoding and video compression. I have worked on problems related to video segmentation, scene flow and texture segmentation. I have been exposed through research or courses to Machine Learning, Video/Image Processing and Convex Optimization.
